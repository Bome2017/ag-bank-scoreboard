Public Access

The public dashboard is provided for informational, research, and non-commercial use. It displays structural viability (G) and performed-health divergence (MHI) for every FDIC-insured bank, along with validation highlights and current-quarter monitoring.

Public access does not grant rights to commercial reuse, redistribution, bulk extraction, automated scraping, or integration into institutional decision systems.

Commercial Use

Commercial use of the A/G Scoreboard, its displayed outputs, and any expanded data products requires a commercial license or prior written permission.

  • Institutional use in risk, trading, lending, or advisory workflows
  • Bulk extraction or automated scraping of dashboard outputs
  • Redistribution of outputs to clients, subscribers, or internal teams
  • Use of expanded ledger outputs, recurring deliveries, or bespoke reporting for commercial purposes

Where included in scope, institutional delivery may include both canonical Min-G and companion Mean-G outputs together with documented temporal features.

Possible Access Types

  • Public dashboard: free, informational, non-commercial
  • One-time custom package: quoted on request
  • Recurring quarterly delivery: quoted on request
  • Institutional / commercial license: quoted on request

For a fuller overview of available access paths and scoped offerings, see the Products & Services page.

Institutional delivery structure, field definitions, temporal features, and optional dual-feed reporting of both Min-G and Mean-G are described in the Institutional Delivery Schema & Data Dictionary.

Scope, permitted use, and price depend on the requested access level and intended use.
